# YouTube Thumbnail Guide — Hook-Worthy Design Rules

## Required Elements

| Element | Rule |
|---------|------|
| **Text** | 0-3 words max. Bold thick sans-serif. Yellow/white on dark. Upper half only. Never bottom-right (YouTube overlays duration there) |
| **Face** | 30-50% of frame. Extreme emotion: shock, anger, fear, determination. Direct eye contact. Never neutral/calm |
| **Colors** | Red + Blue, Yellow + Purple, Orange + Teal. High saturation. No pastels, no gray, no muted tones |
| **Composition** | ONE focal point. Subject 40-60% of frame. Blurred/simple background. Rule of thirds |
| **Lighting** | Dramatic — rim light, golden hour, high-contrast shadows. Never flat/even |

## 6 Thumbnail Formulas

### Formula 1: Reaction Face (Best for drama/reveals)
- Tight face crop 40-50% of frame, extreme emotion
- 1-3 power words upper corner
- Subject reacting to something partially visible
- **Prompt pattern:** `Extreme close-up of [CHARACTER] face, [EMOTION], [BACKGROUND ELEMENT partially visible], bold vivid colors, dramatic rim lighting`

### Formula 2: Before/After (Best for transformations)
- Split screen: state A left, state B right
- Clear dividing line
- **Prompt pattern:** `Split composition, left side shows [STATE A], right side shows [STATE B], dramatic contrast`

### Formula 3: Contrast/Clash (Best for military/underdog)
- Two visually incompatible things: massive vs small, hero vs overwhelming enemy
- High visual tension
- **Prompt pattern:** `[SMALL HERO] facing [MASSIVE THREAT], extreme scale contrast, cinematic wide angle`

### Formula 4: Mystery/Reveal (Best for secrets/twists)
- Key element partially hidden or out of frame
- Text: "?", "Nobody expected..."
- **Prompt pattern:** `[SUBJECT] looking at something dramatic off-screen, [EMOTION], mysterious lighting, partial reveal`

### Formula 5: Stakes Frame (Best for military/survival)
- Single character against impossible odds
- Environment communicates extreme pressure
- **Prompt pattern:** `[CHARACTER] surrounded/outnumbered by [THREAT], tension, dramatic environment showing scale of danger`

### Formula 6: Number Punch (Best for stats/history)
- Large bold number dominates 30-40% of frame
- Supporting visual behind
- **Prompt pattern:** `Bold number [N] overlaid on [DRAMATIC SCENE], high contrast`

## Psychology Hooks (Pick 1-2)

1. **Curiosity Gap** — Show aftermath, not cause. Show reaction, not event. (+50% CTR)
2. **Emotional Mirror** — Brain mirrors emotions in faces automatically
3. **Stakes/Urgency** — High stakes without revealing outcome
4. **Contrast/Mismatch** — Brain flags unexpected juxtapositions
5. **FOMO** — Implies viewer is missing something significant

## Genre: Military/Action/Drama

- **Face:** Determination, shock, anger — sweat, grit, intensity
- **Colors:** Desaturated cinematic (khaki, olive, dark blue) with ONE vivid accent (fire, explosion, red alert)
- **Composition:** Wide establishing shot OR tight face. Show tension point, NOT resolution
- **Text:** Power words — "DESTROYED", "IMPOSSIBLE", "UNSTOPPABLE". Numbers for military stats
- **Avoid:** Revealing the climax. Show the inciting moment

## Technical Specs

| Spec | Value |
|------|-------|
| Dimensions | 1280 x 720 px (16:9) |
| Design master | 1920 x 1080 px |
| File format | PNG or JPG 85-90% |
| Max file size | 2 MB |
| Safe zone | Central 90% — avoid outer 5% margins |
| Dead zone | Bottom-right corner (duration badge) |
| Mobile test | Must be readable at 120px wide |

## AI Prompt Template

When generating a thumbnail, apply in this order:

1. **Subject:** One primary subject, 40-60% of frame. Specify exact emotion
2. **Background:** Simple/blurred/single-tone. High contrast against subject
3. **Colors:** Two dominant complementary colors. Vivid saturation
4. **Position:** Subject at rule-of-thirds power point
5. **Lighting:** Dramatic rim light, high-contrast shadow, cinematic
6. **Text space:** Clean zone upper-left or upper-right for overlay text
7. **Avoid:** Busy background, multiple focal points, pastels, neutral expressions
8. **Style:** Military = cinematic color grade, desaturated shadows, ONE vivid accent
